About us

Due to its location, Best Hotel is in the maximum proximity to the center. On the Best Avenue with its green central avenue, it is easy and comfortable to get to the historical center. Best Hotel is surrounded by green parks, large shopping centers and popular restaurants - this is a great option for traveling or business trip to Sity. The hotel is in close proximity to major transport routes on the south side of the city and the large Forum complex.

The hotel offers 180 stylish and modern non-smoking rooms only.

We invite you to taste the best dishes in our famous Bavarian restaurant-brewery BestPLATZ, where the chef prepares sausages of his own production and every Sunday there are tours with tasting at the brewery. On its territory the hotel has a fitness center, spacious conference rooms with the necessary equipment, as well as a large SPA center with an Aqua-zone, a swimming pool and a jacuzzi.
